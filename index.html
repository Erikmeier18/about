Erik Meier Valdés

Postdoctoral research fellow
Department of Physics, University of Oxford

Characterising hot rocky exoplanets with space-based telescopes like JWST and CHEOPS. I'm particularly interested on 55 Cnc e, a super-Earth orbiting a Sun-like star every 17 hours.

Member of the CHEOPS science team
Member of the JEST collaboration
CO-I (PI: Dr. Hannah Diamond-Lowe) and champion of a target in The Hot Rocks Survey JWST program (GO 3730)
Co-organiser of the SPIMAX seminar series at the University of Oxford

Co-founder of the Exoplanet Journal Club at the University of Bern
